The 5th Workshop on generalized Baire spaces will take place on February 3-4, 2020 at the School of Mathematics, University of Bristol. The workshop will beginn on Monday at 9am and end on Tuesday at 4pm.

The focus of this year's workshop is on connections with model theory and infinite combinatorics. The invited speakers include David Aspero (UEA), Vincenzo Dimonte (Udine), Lorenzo Galeotti (Amsterdam), Philipp Lücke (Bonn), Miguel Moreno (Bar-Ilan), Luca Motto Ros (Torino), Sarka Stejskalova (Prague), and Dorottya Sziraki (Budapest).

The local organizers are Philipp Schlicht and Philip Welch.

The meeting is open to everyone. If you intend to participate or have a question about the workshop, please email Philipp Schlicht (philipp.schlicht@bristol.ac.uk). There is a conference fee of 20 GBP for those participating in the whole meeting.

We have a few slots for contributed 20 minutes talks. If you intend to give one, please send an email soon to secure a slot.
