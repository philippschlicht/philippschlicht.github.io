The 5th Workshop on generalized Baire spaces will take place on February 3-4, 2020 at the School of Mathematics, University of Bristol (Monday at 9am -- Tuesday 4pm).

The focus of this year's workshop is on connections with model theory and infinite combinatorics.

The invited speakers are David Aspero (Norwich), Vincenzo Dimonte (Udine), Lorenzo Galeotti (Amsterdam), Philipp Lücke (Bonn), Miguel Moreno (Bar-Ilan), Luca Motto Ros (Torino), Isabel Müller (London), Sarka Stejskalova (Prague), and Dorottya Sziraki (Budapest).

The meeting is open to everyone. If you intend to participate or have a question about the workshop, please email Philipp Schlicht (philipp.schlicht@bristol.ac.uk). There is a conference fee of 20 GBP for those participating in the whole meeting.

We have a few slots for contributed 20-25 minutes talks. If you intend to give one, please send an email soon to secure a slot.

The meeting is funded by a London Mathematical Society Scheme 1 grant, Marie Curie Individual Fellowship 794020 and the British Logic Colloquium. The local organizers are Dan Nielsen, Philipp Schlicht, Christopher Turner and Philip Welch.
