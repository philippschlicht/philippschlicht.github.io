5th Workshop on generalized Baire spaces, 3rd-4th of February 2020, University of Bristol

The 5th Workshop on generalized Baire spaces will take place on 3rd-4th February 2020 (Monday 9am to Tuesday 4pm) at the School of Mathematics of the University of Bristol. The focus of this year's workshop is on connections with model theory and infinite combinatorics.

The main speakers are David Aspero (Norwich), Vincenzo Dimonte (Udine), Lorenzo Galeotti (Amsterdam), Philipp Lücke (Bonn), Miguel Moreno (Bar-Ilan), Luca Motto Ros (Torino), Isabel Müller (London), Sarka Stejskalova (Prague), and Dorottya Sziraki (Budapest).

We have a few slots for contributed 20-25 minutes talks. If you intend to give one, please send an email soon to secure a slot.

The meeting is open to everyone, with a conference fee of 20 GBP for those participating in the whole meeting. If you intend to participate or have questions, please email Philipp Schlicht (philipp.schlicht@bristol.ac.uk). The local organizers are Dan Nielsen, Philipp Schlicht, Christopher Turner and Philip Welch.

The meeting is funded by a London Mathematical Society Scheme 1 grant, Marie Curie Individual Fellowship 794020 and the British Logic Colloquium.
