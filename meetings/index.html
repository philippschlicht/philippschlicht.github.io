Set Theory in the United Kingdom (STUK 2) will take place Wednesday, 8 May 2019 at the School of Mathematics of the University of Bristol. STUK 1 took place in Cambridge on 16 February 2019.

Program

11-12 Second order set theory (Victoria Gitman) 12-13 Lunch at the math department 13-14 Set theory and category theory (Andrew Brooke-Taylor) 14-15 10 minute presentations of open problems 15-16 Discussions 16-16.30 Coffee and cake 16.30-18 Discussions The talks are introductory and will lead to discussions of open questions in the afternoon. There will be ample time for collaboration and discussions.
